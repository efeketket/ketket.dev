## **CUSTOMIZING SHOP LOOKİNG**
![d](https://github.com/efeketket/ketkets-player-shops/assets/117019767/11beba05-896a-4ee2-a843-c01f5dcff255)

* As a owner of the player shop, you can customize your shops looking.
* Change Glass Color : Shift+Right Click with empty hand to glass.
* Change Base Plate : Shift+Right Click with whatever block you want to change to baseplate.
* Change Wool Color : Shift+Right Click with empty hand to baseplate. (you can remove or change the color of wool.)
